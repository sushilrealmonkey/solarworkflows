Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Bizlee handles information you share through this website, including demo and pricing requests.

Information we collect

When you request pricing or book a demo, we may collect information such as your name, business email, mobile number, company name, city, monthly enquiry volume, and preferred demo date and time. We may also receive basic technical information that browsers and hosting services normally record, such as device type, browser type, and IP address.

How we use information

  • To respond to enquiries and arrange product demonstrations.
  • To provide pricing, onboarding, support, and product-related communication.
  • To operate, secure, understand, and improve the website and Bizlee services.
  • To meet legal obligations and prevent misuse or fraud.

How information is shared

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us host the website, process form submissions, communicate with you, or operate Bizlee. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect rights and safety, or as part of a business reorganization.

Third-party services and links

This website may use third-party form, hosting, analytics, or communication services. Those providers process information under their own terms and privacy practices. Links to third-party websites are provided for convenience; Bizlee is not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

Data retention and security

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, including support, business, security, and legal needs. We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your choices

You may ask to access, correct, or delete personal information you have submitted, or opt out of non-essential communications. Some information may be retained where required for legal, security, or legitimate business purposes.

Children’s privacy

Bizlee is intended for businesses and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website.

Updates to this policy

We may update this policy as our website, services, or legal obligations change. The effective date at the top of this page identifies the latest version.
